See our work as an invitation. Through art in public space, we invite people into a conversation, offering our inner world while responding to theirs. This is not about creating finished pieces or isolated artworks—it is social and cultural work, where dialogue, reflection, and shared experience are at the heart of the practice.

By situating this practice in public space, artists open themselves to interaction and connection. Public space becomes both canvas and collaborator—a place where art and everyday life meet. OUPS! is an invitation to think, question, respond, and co-create meaning together. In this sense, art becomes a living conversation shaped by everyone who engages with it.

All projects are created and carried out independently by the artists. OUPS! Festival simply acts as an open invitation and platform for artists to share their work in public space. Each artist is responsible for their own actions.
