À propos

We see our work as an invitation. Through art in public space, we invite people into a conversation, oﬀering our inner world while responding to theirs. This is not about creating finished pieces or isolated artworks—it is social and cultural work, where dialogue, reflection, and shared experience are at the heart of the practice. By situating our practice in public space, we open ourselves to interaction and connection. We aim to resonate with people and, in turn, be resonated by them. Public space becomes both our canvas and our collaborator, a place where art and life meet. OUPS! is an invitation to think, question, respond, and co-create meaning together. In public space, art becomes a living conversation, and everyone who engages contributes to its unfolding.
